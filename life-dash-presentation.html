Production Notes

Stills → Video Rules

  • Diptych: animate with dissolve, wipe, or data-populate
  • Graph close-up: build line by line on screen
  • Flat lay: timelapse or slow push-in
  • Person + phone: add micro-reaction (relief, quiet nod)
  • Paper chaos: physical-to-digital morph

Casting

  • Ages 35–65
  • No model-perfect bodies
  • No fitness-aspirational framing
  • Maximum demographic diversity across all 9 executions

Compliance

  • Do not name conditions in GLP-1 ads (Meta policy)
  • Lead with whole-health language, not disease framing
  • No before/after body images in GLP-1 paths
  • All CTAs lead with "Free" — no hidden cost signals
